# REPLACED - Official Website

Official website for **REPLACED**, a stunning 2.5D sci-fi retro-futuristic platformer combining cinematic action, pixel art, and free flow combat. Launching March 12, 2026.

**Website**: [replacedgame.com](https://replacedgame.com)

## About REPLACED

REPLACED is a 2.5D sci-fi retro-futuristic platformer that tells a deep, engaging dystopian story where you play as R.E.A.C.H, an artificial intelligence trapped in a human body against its own will, striving to adjust to human life in and around Phoenix-City.

### Key Features

- **Cinematic 2.5D Platforming**: Experience stunning pixel art graphics with modern cinematic presentation
- **Free Flow Combat**: Master fluid combat mechanics with chained attacks, dodges, and special abilities
- **Deep Narrative**: Explore themes of identity, consciousness, and what it means to be human
- **Phoenix-City**: Navigate through a beautifully crafted cyberpunk metropolis filled with secrets and challenges
- **Alternative 1980s Setting**: Immerse yourself in a retro-futuristic world where past and future collide
