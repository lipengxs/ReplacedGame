# REPLACED SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 107 clicks, 9,645 impressions, 1.11% CTR, average position 12.24. News is the strongest template; system requirements, patch notes and screenshots have top-10 opportunity.

## 0–30 days

- Retain the fan-site disclosure and add visible source and last-checked information to release, patch and platform claims.
- Create a dedicated technical requirements page, separating official minimum/recommended requirements from editorial performance advice.
- Ensure each patch entry has a permanent URL, source, platform scope and player-impact summary.

## 31–60 days

- Build original pages for collectibles, combat, settings, demo differences and screenshot/media discovery.
- Archive older delay news so it does not conflict with the current release status.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Track patch-page CTR and official-store outbound clicks; refresh only on verified updates.
- Target technical-page CTR above 2% and make news/patch pages deliver more than half of organic clicks.
