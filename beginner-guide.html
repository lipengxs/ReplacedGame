REPLACED Beginner Guide

REPLACED is a focused cinematic action platformer. Beginners should learn rhythm, spacing, environmental awareness, and replay tools instead of expecting RPG-style build planning.

1. Learn The Combat Loop

Every fight is easier when you keep the loop simple: observe, dodge or reposition, punish, then reset. Do not mash through enemy windups.

  • Watch attack timing
  • Use short punish windows
  • Back off after unsafe hits

2. Explore Phoenix-City

Read the level like a cinematic scene. Light, background motion, and room layout often tell you where to look next.

  • Separate story path from cleanup
  • Watch platforming hazards
  • Revisit combat arenas with level select

3. Protect The Mood

R.E.A.C.H.'s premise is central, so avoid heavy spoilers before finishing major sections. Use spoiler-free story notes first.

  • Track identity and memory themes
  • Save lore notes for later
  • Use dialogue skip only on replay

4. Prepare Your PC

Steam lists Windows 10 64-bit, 16 GB RAM, GTX 1060 6GB or equivalent, and 8 GB storage.

Check PC specs or compare similar games.
