Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to REPLACED. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and play REPLACED, the 2.5D sci-fi retro-futuristic platformer developed by Sad Cat Studios and published by Thunderful Games, launching on March 12, 2026.

Information We Collect

When you use REPLACED, we may collect:

  • Game Data: Information about your progress as R.E.A.C.H, gameplay statistics, achievements, collectibles found in Phoenix-City, and in-game activities
  • Account Information: Your game account details, username, email address, and contact information
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, platform information (Xbox, Steam, Epic Games Store), and network connection details
  • Usage Data: Gameplay duration, feature usage patterns, combat statistics, and exploration progress

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and enhance your REPLACED gaming experience
  • Save your progress through Phoenix-City and gameplay data
  • Implement game balance adjustments and improvements to combat and platforming mechanics
  • Process in-game purchases and transactions (if applicable)
  • Prevent cheating and inappropriate behavior
  • Send game updates and event notifications about REPLACED
  • Provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to improve the REPLACED experience

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and:

  • Employ industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • Strictly control data access permissions
  • Conduct regular security audits and updates
  • Never sell your personal information to third parties
  • Store data in secure, encrypted databases
  • Comply with applicable data protection regulations

Minor Protection

To protect minors playing REPLACED, we implement:

  • Age verification system for appropriate content access
  • Spending restrictions for minors
  • Playtime management system
  • Parental control features
  • Safe content filters and monitoring

Policy Updates

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be communicated through in-game announcements or website notifications at replacedgame.com. Continued use of REPLACED constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • Official Website: playreplaced.com
  • Twitter: @ReplacedGame
  • Sad Cat Studios: sadcatstudios.com
  • Thunderful Games: thunderfulgames.com
