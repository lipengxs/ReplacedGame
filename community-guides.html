REPLACED Community Guide Digest

REPLACED community discussion has been especially useful around combat feel and replay features. This page turns those conversations into a clean checklist for guide updates.

Combat Feedback Themes

  • Players often report better results when they stop chasing damage and focus on spacing.
  • Dodge timing is a rhythm tool, not just an emergency escape.
  • Ranged enemies should be handled early in multi-enemy rooms.
  • Over-committing to attack strings makes fights feel chaotic.

Replay Feature Notes

Community feedback and official updates point toward replay convenience: level select, dialogue skip, letterbox settings, and collectible tracking. These should be summarized as replay tools, not first-playthrough shortcuts.

Guide Update Ideas

  • Enemy priority table by room type.
  • Dodge timing notes with safe reset examples.
  • Level-select cleanup checklist.
  • Spoiler-safe route summaries for Phoenix-City.
