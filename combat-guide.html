REPLACED Combat Guide

REPLACED combat is less about grinding numbers and more about rhythm. Read the windup, move with intent, punish only when safe, then reset before the next mistake.

Core Loop

  • Observe the enemy's first movement.
  • Dodge or step out of the line of attack.
  • Use a short punish instead of a greedy combo.
  • Reset spacing before the next windup.

Arena Reads

Rooms are staged like scenes. Check where the camera wants your eye, where hazards sit, and whether the foreground blocks a safe retreat.

Practice Plan

  • Use level select to repeat one hard encounter.
  • Skip dialogue only after your first run.
  • Practice defensive clears before fast clears.
  • Write down which enemy starts the chain reaction.

Common Mistakes

Most deaths come from panic dodging, chasing after a missed hit, or ignoring the second enemy while finishing the first. Pair this page with the Phoenix-City route guide.
